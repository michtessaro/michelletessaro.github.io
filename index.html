Bridging Research to Practice to Improve Social Equity

Practice & Research

Drawing on more than two decades of executive leadership and evidence‑based research to address structural inequities across the leadership pipeline, including the broken rung and the lack of women in senior leadership roles such as CEO.

AI is a new area of focus, recognising how its rapid advancement could either entrench existing barriers or enable genuine structural change.

Focus Areas

Structural Inequity

Identifying and dismantling the systemic barriers that prevent women from progressing beyond the first step of management into senior leadership.

AI: Empowering Change or Entrenching Bias

Investigating the dual-nature of algorithmic evolution in the corporate sphere and its long-term impact on leadership diversity.
